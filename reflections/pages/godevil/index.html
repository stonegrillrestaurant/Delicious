The Architect Within

We look to the heavens for salvation and the shadows for blame,
yet the architect of both resides within.

We are conditioned to project our virtues onto divinity and our vices onto demons, a grand illusion that tricks our focus outward.

In truth, good and evil are not warring deities; they are the fundamental elements of our own nature. You are the vessel. What you personify is not fate—it is purely a matter of choice.

Listen to the Reflection
Acknowledge the shadow. Choose the light.
