How to Order

  1. Visit stonegrillresto.net/order
  2. Select your food category and click “Add” beside the items you want.
  3. Check your cart and adjust quantities as needed.
  4. Fill in your details (Name, Mobile, Order Type, Date & Time, Special Requests).
  5. Click “Place Order” to send it to our staff.
  6. Wait for our confirmation and payment instructions.
  7. Pay via GCash or in-store before we process your order.

For faster service, prepare your payment after placing the order.
