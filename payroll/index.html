Stone Grill Payroll

Stone Grill Payroll – Smart Cloud Suite is a secure cloud-based payroll and employee management system developed for Stone Grill Restaurant.

Purpose of the Application

Stone Grill Payroll is designed to help authorized administrators manage employee payroll, attendance records, cash advances, incentives, and payslip generation for internal business operations.

This application is for authorized Stone Grill Restaurant personnel only.

Application Features

  • Employee attendance tracking
  • Payroll computation
  • Payslip generation
  • Cash advance and deduction records
  • Bonus and incentive monitoring
  • Administrative payroll reports

Privacy and Terms

Stone Grill Payroll uses secure authentication and is intended only for payroll and employee management purposes.
