Eza Commercial

The Socio-Economic Nexus of Bato, Leyte

Institutional Identity

Vision

Eza Commercial stands as a cornerstone of the commercial landscape in southwestern Leyte. Located on J. Luna Street in Barangay Iniguihan, the municipality of Bato, the enterprise serves as a vital conduit for both physical trade and digital financial services.

Under the dedicated individual proprietorship of Flordeliza A. Lopez, Eza Commercial has evolved from a traditional retail outlet into a sophisticated node of regional economic integration.

The enterprise's strategic location near the Bato port allows it to leverage maritime logistical flows, ensuring a steady supply of essential goods for the local population. This geographical advantage is paired with an operational philosophy centered on consistency and accessibility.

Core Operations

Retail and Wholesale Distribution

As a general merchandise provider, Eza Commercial caters to a broad spectrum of needs, ranging from daily household staples to agricultural supplies.

It plays a critical role in the local supply chain as a wholesale distributor, providing the necessary inventory for smaller "sari-sari" stores located in the remote interior barangays of both Bato and Hilongos. By maintaining a diverse product mix, the business acts as a stabilizing force for the municipal economy, ensuring that essential commodities remain available even during periods of seasonal fluctuation.

Innovation

A Frontier of Financial Inclusion

The most defining characteristic of Eza Commercial’s recent history is its transformation into a vital financial hub. By partnering with major banking institutions, the enterprise provides "last mile" financial services to an underserved community.

Decentralized Banking

Through its participation in the RCBC ATMGo and BancNet Cash Out networks, Eza Commercial functions as a decentralized banking point where residents can perform cash withdrawals, check balances, and transfer funds seamlessly.

Social Welfare Distribution

Serving as an authorized partner for Landbankasama, we facilitate the distribution of government subsidies to beneficiaries. This synergy between retail and agency banking makes Eza an indispensable utility for the modern resident.
