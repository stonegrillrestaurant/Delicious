The Diet of the Spirit

Topic: Spiritual Junk Food

The soul is the spiritual part of our body that grows with us along the way. It is not something that enters our body at birth as a finished product, but something that is created and expands depending on what you feed it with.

[attachment_0](attachment)

Just as our bones lengthen through physical nourishment, our soul expands through the metabolism of wisdom, confidence, and compassion. When we say a soul is "lost," we often mean it has been starved of the correct spiritual nutrients.

"Are we really feeding our souls right, or are we just sedating them with the junk food of dogma and fear?"

The Spiritual Audit

Use the table below to evaluate what you are currently feeding your spirit:

Spiritual Junk Food The Impact The Soul Food
Fear of Punishment Creates a brittle, anxious spirit. Universal Compassion
Blind Dogma Stunts intellectual and spiritual wisdom. Self-Reflection

Ultimately, the alchemy of the soul is about turning the lead of fear into the gold of understanding. It is an active process that requires us to be mindful of our spiritual intake.
