The God We Created

A reflection on projection, divinity, and freedom.

▶ The God We Created 4:12
▶ Unmasking Podcast 8:45
▶ Deep Dive Discussion 6:20

The Reflection

For centuries, we were told that we are the center of creation. But the stars tell a different story. If this universe is so vast, so unreachable—was it truly made for us?

Perhaps we created God in our image—before He ever created us in His. Maybe the God of fire and punishment was not the eternal source, but a reflection of our own fears.

The true God waits. Within. In stillness. In wonder.
